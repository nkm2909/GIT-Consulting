Who We Are

GIT Consulting Pty Ltd is an Australian-origin services and solutions company headquartered in Melbourne, delivering ServiceNow AI implementation, custom software development, cloud solutions, and digital transformation programs for enterprises across the globe.

With a deep focus on the Telecom and FinTech sectors, our consultants bring extensive hands-on experience delivering complex enterprise technology programs across Australia and India.

Through our delivery centre in Melbourne and Pune, India, we offer cost-effective capability without compromising on quality — enabling us to scale quickly while maintaining the high standards our Australian clients expect.
