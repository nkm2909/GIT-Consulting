Executive Profile

Neeraj Kumar Mishra is a visionary technology leader and Co-Founder of GIT Consulting Pty Ltd, bringing over 17 years of global IT experience to Australia's enterprise technology landscape. As Director of Technology Services, Neeraj leads the firm's strategic direction across ServiceNow platform delivery, Agentic AI implementation, and digital transformation for complex enterprise environments.

A strategic thinker with an exceptionally strong technical foundation, Neeraj has built a reputation for delivering high-quality, scalable software solutions and for fostering high-performing engineering teams across Australia and India. His work spans organisations of all scales — from fast-growing technology companies to some of Australia's most critical national infrastructure providers.

ServiceNow & AI Expertise

Neeraj is one of Australia's most experienced ServiceNow solution architects, with deep hands-on implementation and governance expertise across the entire ServiceNow ecosystem. His technical depth spans ITSM, CSM, TSM, ITOM, Vulnerability Management, GRC, and IRM — including advanced work in Now Assist AI capabilities, Agentic AI workflows, and the emerging ServiceNow AI Control Tower framework.

Recognised as an early adopter and practitioner of Generative AI on the ServiceNow platform, Neeraj has led complex Now Assist implementations and architected Agentic AI solutions that deliver measurable outcomes — from intelligent ticket triage and auto-resolution to AI-generated risk narratives and autonomous compliance monitoring.

Leadership Philosophy

Neeraj believes that great technology outcomes are only possible when deep technical expertise is paired with genuine curiosity about people and process. He is passionate about building solutions that create real-world impact — not just technically impressive ones — and takes particular pride in developing the next generation of platform engineers and AI practitioners across GIT Consulting's Melbourne and Pune teams.

Active in Australia's ServiceNow community, Neeraj regularly engages with industry forums, ServiceNow World Forum events, and the broader enterprise AI ecosystem — staying at the forefront of Now Assist evolution, Agentic AI frameworks, and responsible AI implementation practices for Australian organisations.
