Executive Profile

Neeraj Kumar Mishra is a seasoned technology executive, Co-Founder and Director of GIT Consulting Pty Ltd, with over 18 years of extensive global experience leading and driving large-scale transformation programs across IT service management, Telco customer assurance platforms, platform engineering, observability, and the establishment of high-performance software engineering teams.

A strategic thinker with an exceptionally strong technical foundation, Neeraj has operated at the intersection of enterprise architecture, engineering leadership, and C-level advisory — collaborating with Chief Executives, GMs, and technology boards to align technology strategy with commercial outcomes. His work spans some of Australia's most complex enterprise environments, including national broadband infrastructure and one of Australia's largest telecommunications enterprises.

Across his career, Neeraj has been recognised with two prestigious industry awards — the CIO Award (2018) and the Direct Connect Top 100 Influential Leaders (2020), the latter based entirely on peer nomination — reflecting the trust and lasting impact he has built across engineering, product, and executive communities throughout his programs.

ServiceNow Platform & AI Expertise

Neeraj is one of Australia's most experienced ServiceNow architects and engineering leaders, with deep hands-on expertise spanning the full ServiceNow ecosystem. Over more than a decade of ServiceNow delivery, he has led the consolidation of 20+ enterprise assurance platforms into a single ServiceNow instance, governed 10 concurrent ServiceNow instances across a major telco, defined CMDB strategy that reduced asset management errors by 40%, and optimised platform performance by 20% through targeted database tuning and best-practice architecture.

As an early practitioner of Generative AI in enterprise environments, Neeraj led the proof-of-concept and initial rollout of Azure AI to pilot engineering teams — a programme projected to deliver $1.5M in productivity savings over two years. He brings this hands-on AI delivery experience directly to GIT Consulting's ServiceNow Now Assist and Agentic AI practice.

Observability & Platform Engineering

Neeraj has built a distinctive and in-demand specialism in enterprise observability and platform engineering. Across his enterprise programs, he architected and signed off observability platform blueprints that delivered approximately $4M in OPEX savings over three years — rationalising fragmented toolchains into coherent, governed observability strategies. He has also driven the architecture for CPaaS (Communication Platform as a Service) rationalisation, saving a further $1.6M annually.

His platform engineering leadership includes the design and execution of the Golden Path initiative — enforcing coding standards, improving developer experience, and reducing build costs by 20% and operational costs by 15% across large-scale engineering organisations. He established Centres of Excellence for assurance engineering teams across Australia and India, lifting team maturity scores from 2 to 4.5 out of 5, and embedded T-shaped skills and DevSecOps left-shift practices to reduce security risk and costly post-production defects.

IT OPEX Optimisation & Commercial Impact

Few technology leaders combine deep architectural capability with a genuine commercial lens. Neeraj has a consistent record of identifying and realising significant IT operational expense savings — across platform consolidation, vendor negotiation, cloud rationalisation, and engineering efficiency programs. His achievements include optimising over $3M in ServiceNow licence costs through rigorous vendor due diligence, rationalising CPaaS and observability platforms to collectively save more than $5.6M in OPEX, and driving developer productivity improvements projected to deliver $1.5M in AI-assisted savings.

Across his enterprise programs, he held delivery ownership and budget management responsibility for multiple concurrent major initiatives — spanning developer experience, testing transformation, and ServiceNow consolidation — while overseeing vendor relationships and capital funding approval across engineering organisations of over 300 people.

B2B Integration & Enterprise Architecture

Neeraj has designed and delivered enterprise-scale B2B integration programs, integrating large-scale customer assurance platforms with major enterprise customers — improving end-to-end incident, service request, asset, problem, and change management across complex ecosystems. He conceptualised and executed an ITaaS (IT as a Service) API strategy across an assurance business portfolio, productised the B2B integration pattern to reduce revenue leakage, and aligned multi-platform architectures across Salesforce, Aria, Amdocs, and ServiceNow.
